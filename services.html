Practice 01

Water Conservation & Recharge Structures

End-to-end solutions focused on water security, conservation, and ecosystem restoration — engineered for India's diverse hydrology.

Pond & Water Body Restoration
De-silting, embankment strengthening, inlet/outlet design and landscaping.
Rainwater Harvesting Systems
Rooftop, surface and recharge well systems for residential, industrial and institutional sites.
Wastewater Treatment Solutions
Conventional STPs and decentralised DEWATS systems with reuse integration.
Watershed Management & Check Dams
Catchment-scale interventions to slow, store and recharge surface runoff.

Afforestation & Ecological Development

Creating resilient green landscapes that enhance biodiversity, restore degraded land, and re-introduce native species to the everyday city.

Afforestation & Plantation Drives
Site-appropriate native species, structured plantation cycles, and long-term care.
Miyawaki Forest Development
Dense native multi-layered forests grown 10× faster on a fraction of the land.
Urban Green Spaces, Rain Gardens & Parks
Bioswales, infiltration beds, pollinator strips and people-first park design.
Thematic & Biodiversity Gardens
Educational and biodiversity-rich spaces for schools, campuses and townships.
